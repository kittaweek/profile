# About Me

I started out as a regular software developer, building websites with PHP and WordPress before expanding into Laravel, Magento, and the modern JavaScript era. Along the way, I have always worked on infrastructure in parallel—from managing Linux servers and email systems to building CI/CD pipelines, Observability stacks, and Docker containers. This hands-on experience allows me to view systems from both developer and DevOps perspectives. As my experience grew, I stepped up to lead a small team, learning both deep technical software development and team management in parallel.

When the data era arrived, I decided to pivot to Data Engineering and Data Analysis to broaden my perspective on data management. However, I ultimately realized that building software is where my true passion lies. I have since returned to software engineering full-time as a Software Engineer at Connex Property. Alongside my main role, I continuously study and experiment with AI models and automation systems, constantly reviewing and expanding my knowledge base.

My insatiable curiosity and genuine love for technology are my primary driving forces. I excel at logical problem-solving, maintain an open mind, and am always ready to adapt. To me, every challenge is an opportunity to learn—I never wait to be fully prepared before diving in. In my free time, you will always find me working on personal side projects and taking online courses to continuously level up my skills (honestly, my mind is currently overflowing with so many project ideas that I can't keep up!).

When I'm away from the screen, I enjoy relaxing at good coffee shops, listening to insightful podcasts to acquire new knowledge, going out for a run, organizing camping trips in nature, or having fun playing board games and PC games with friends.
